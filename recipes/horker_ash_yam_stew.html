Horker & Ash Yam Stew

Made with the finest of Horker meats available and the freshest of veggies from the harsh frozen wastelands of Skyrim - A bowl of this is surely going to make you gain some solid hitpoints and be prepared to fight against the next lot of Draugr on your dungeon crawls!

Ingredients

  • 1 Brown Onion, Finely Chopped
  • 5 Cloves Of Garlic
  • 280 grams Of Horker Meat
  • 1 litre Veg Stock
  • 2 Tbsp Paprika Powder
  • 1 Tbsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 Tbsp Chili Powder
  • 1/2 Tbsp Curry Powder
  • Salt and Pepper To Taste
  • Mixed Dry Herbs
  • 1.5 Large Purple Yams, Peeled and Chopped Large
  • 1 Potato, Peeled And Cubed
  • 100 mL Coconut Cream
  • 1 Large Leek, Sliced
  • 1 Large Carrot, Peeled And Sliced

Steps

  1. Prepare the stock and the veggies
  2. Fry the meat until tender, then add the veggies and the spices
  3. let it reduce and add the stock
  4. add salt and pepper to taste
  5. serve hot
