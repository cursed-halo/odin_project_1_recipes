Jazbay Crostata

Ahh, the sweet taste of a Jazbay Crostata. Here in this recipe you'll find the absolutely easiest way to forego the troubles of stealing crostata off of random inns and make your own!

Ingredients

  • 250 Grams Of Flour
  • 134 Grams Of Butter, Cold And Diced
  • 25 Grams Of Castor Sugar
  • Pinch Of Salt
  • 3 Large Whole Eggs
  • 3 Table Spoons Milk
  • 0.5 Cup Mammoth Cheese
  • 1 Table Spoon Lemon Juice
  • 1 Table Spoon Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Table Spoon Heavy Cream
  • 3 Table Spoons Sugar
  • Some Extra Melted Butter, For Brushing

Steps

  1. Make The Dough By Mixing In All Ingredients.
  2. Bake For A Pretty Long Time
  3. Ponder Why The Fuck Does A Jazbay Crostata Not Have Jazbays In It
  4. Get Attacked By Draugr
  5. Get Your Crostata Fresh Out Of The Stove, Butter It And Eat It To Regain HP
  6. Muder Aforementioned Draugr
